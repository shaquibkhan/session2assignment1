GREEN TEA

As we all know that green tea is exceptionally useful for weight loss but many few of us aware from the fact that its very rich in antioxidant.

Ingredients

  • dry green leaves
  • luke warm water
  • fresh lemon

Method

  1. Firstly boil water at room temperature.
  2. Then pour a spoon of green leaf for making 1 cup tea & keep it covered for a minute.
  3. Then strain it in a cup.
  4. Add couples of drop of fresh lemon if want to add some taste to it.
  5. Now ready to serve.
